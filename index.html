About

Financial markets represent perhaps the most challenging frontier for machine learning: an environment characterized by extreme non-stationarity, low signal-to-noise ratios, and high-stakes adversarial dynamics. As the industry shifts toward agentic systems—autonomous entities capable of reasoning and planning—the gap between empirical success and theoretical understanding widens.

  • A unique laboratory for robustness: continual learning under constant distribution shift
  • The vanguard of AI safety & regulation: verification, certified robustness, governed autonomy
  • Agentic systems for mission-critical deployment in finance

XForecast Challenge

Overview

A community challenge on news-grounded financial forecasting, designed to test how modern AI—from reasoning agents to multimodal foundation models—can be operationalized to deconstruct complex market narratives.

  • Dataset: 5 years of LSEG Reuters News aligned with market price/volume
  • Task: out-of-sample forecasting for 2024–2025
  • Prize: $4,500 prize pool
  • Timeline: April–June 2026
