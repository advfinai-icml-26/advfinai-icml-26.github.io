Advances in Financial AI

Agentic, Robust, and Responsible Systems

Financial markets are a stress test for machine learning: extreme non-stationarity, low signal-to-noise ratios, and high-stakes adversarial dynamics. This workshop connects core ML research and financial applications, with a special focus on agentic systems, robustness, and responsible deployment.
