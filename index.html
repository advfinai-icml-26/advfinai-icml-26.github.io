About

Financial markets represent perhaps the most challenging frontier for machine learning: an environment characterized by extreme non-stationarity, low signal-to-noise ratios, and high-stakes adversarial dynamics. As the industry shifts toward agentic systems—autonomous entities capable of reasoning and planning—the gap between empirical success and theoretical understanding widens.

  • A unique laboratory for robustness: continual learning under constant distribution shift
  • The vanguard of AI safety & regulation: verification, certified robustness, governed autonomy
  • Agentic systems for mission-critical deployment in finance

XForecast Challenge

A Kaggle competition on multimodal, news-grounded financial forecasting, organized as part of the ICML 2026 Workshop on Advances in Financial AI.

Join on Kaggle
Overview

The XForecast Challenge explores how modern AI models can combine financial time-series data with unstructured news text to improve stock forecasting. Participants are given historical stock prices together with associated Reuters financial news and are asked to predict future closing prices four weeks ahead.

The challenge focuses on 100 stocks by market capitalization and is designed to encourage research on multimodal learning, representation learning, and robust forecasting methods for financial markets.

Dataset

Historical stock prices paired with Reuters / LSEG financial news.

Modality

Multimodal forecasting with numerical signals and textual context.

Task

Predict stock closing prices 4 weeks ahead.

Evaluation

Ranked by a Hit Rate-based evaluation metric.
